REPUTED & RELIABLE NAME FOR ELECTRICAL STEEL

Established in 1962 at Mumbai and spread its wings in Pune during 1983. “PRAYOG” became a trusted brand in various field of electrical industry. Inclusion of new young management with proven track record of electrical steel for more than two decades taken it to next level by backward integration of electrical steel sector like CRGO, CRNGO and Amorphous technology. Prayog is approved BHEL, PGCIL, NTPC etc & in process of extending to BIS & Railways like organisations. We have state-of-the-art facilities & machines to serve all special needs of our esteemed customers. Manufacturing facility is located at strategic place on Mumbai Bengaluru highway.
